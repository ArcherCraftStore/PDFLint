The Flood From The Desert

The hero Wu Dhin Yii was wandering through the Wooded area near the Plymouth Settlement in Massechusets, determined as ever until a crazy thig occured:  A Wet BOOM! was heard from the forest in Pennsylvania, and the waters began rushing like armies of the Serengheti towards Wu Dhin Yii. The Hero rushed up the nearest Colonial Settlement He could find and little did he know, The colonist who beheaded Metacom was right in front of him...
